Programs Offered

  • The Department of Education's Alternative Learning System (ALS) is a parallel non-formal learning system for out-of-school children, youth, and adults (OSCYAs) in the Philippines. It provides an alternative pathway for individuals who have not completed basic education in the formal system. ALS aims to develop basic and functional literacy skills, offer equivalent pathways to complete basic education, and improve the quality of life for those who have limited access to formal education.
  • The AUTOMOTIVE SERVICING NCI & NCII Qualification consist of competencies that a person must achieve to perform pre-delivery inspection and periodic maintenance of automotive parts and components.
  • The COMPUTER SYSTEMS SERVICING NCII
    Qualification consists of competencies that must possess to enable to install and configure computers systems, set-up computer networks and servers and to maintain and repair computer systems and networks.
  • The BOOKKEEPING NCIII Qualification consists of competencies that a person must achieve to enable him/her to journalize transactions, post transactions, prepare trial balance, prepare financial reports and review internal control system.

What is SGG?

The Sacred Gift of God Training Center (SGG), Inc. was established as a non-stock, non-profit association in the Philippines with a dedicated service mission: to provide and operate vocational and training center, provide training for livelihood, provide technical training for automotive servicing, welding, building wiring and electrical installation, computer servicing and other technical trainings, and also provide educational assistance by providing tuition fees and transportation allowance to the qualified recipients.
